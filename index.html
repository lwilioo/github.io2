The Foundation

Built for the Rugged, Designed for Discovery.

The Expeditionist Hiking Club isn't just a school organization; it's a legacy of exploration. Founded on the principle that true learning happens beyond the classroom walls, we traverse the most challenging terrains to foster leadership, resilience, and environmental stewardship.

Our Mission

To equip students with technical mountaineering skills while cultivating a profound respect for the wild landscapes we inhabit.

Legacy Stats

Over 450 summits reached, 12,000 miles mapped, and a community of 2,000+ alumni since inception.
